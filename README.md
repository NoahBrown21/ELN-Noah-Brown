# Noah Brown's Electronic Notebook

Noah (NoahBrown21) is a Msc student at University College London studying Drug Discovery and Design. He is currently undertaking a research project under the supervision of Prof. Matthew Todd with the Todd group (https://todd-lers.github.io/about/current.html). This ELN contains experiments and observations obtained from the research project. 

The goal of the project is to develop a fluoresence polarisation (FP) assay to identify inhibitors of selected ABHD proteins. Demonstration of assay viability will allow for optimisation of inhibitors, with the goal of developing potent chemical probes to elludicate the biological mechanisms of ABHD protins. 

# ELN Pages 

On the main issues page, issues are either open or closed. Open issues represent planned or ongoing experiments, while closed issues represent completed experiments. 

# Experimental Identifiers 

Experiments within the ELN are identified using the following key:
1. The initials of the experimentalist (NB)
2. Underscore
3. The ABHD protein being tested (ABHD14B)
4. Underscore
5. General method
6. Underscore 
7. The repeat number (03)

For example, NB_ABHD14B_FluoresencePolarisation_03 represents the 3rd repeat of a ABHD14B FP assay
